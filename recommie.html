About This Website

This website is designed for first-year students seeking a personalized approach to learning JavaScript. Since everyone learns differently, the interactive questions help students discover their study habits and assess their programming level. By engaging with these exercises, learners can identify areas for improvement and build a strong foundation in coding. And if this website helps even a little, Recommie is always happy to be part of the journey!

Short for "recommendations," Recommie isn't just a name; it's a friendly presence designed to make learning feel less lonely. Whether you're tackling tough concepts or celebrating small wins, Recommie is here to keep you company and make coding more engaging.
